Transition Policy

Guidelines for changing tutors or ending tuition arrangements.

Changing Tutors

If a student wishes to change tutors, they must notify Nuha Tutors support. We will assist in finding a suitable replacement while ensuring proper closure of the existing arrangement.

Ending Tuition

Either party may end a monthly tuition arrangement with appropriate notice as agreed at confirmation. Outstanding sessions must be completed or adjusted per platform payment policy.

Tutor Transitions

When a tutor cannot continue, they must provide reasonable notice to the student and Nuha Tutors support so a replacement can be arranged without disrupting the student's learning.

Handover

Tutors transitioning out should provide brief progress notes to help incoming tutors continue effectively, subject to student consent.
