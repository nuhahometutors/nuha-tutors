Nuha Tutors Schedule of Charges: Transparency in Pricing & Payments

At Nuha Tutors, we maintain absolute financial transparency. We believe that a clear understanding of platform fees, payment timelines, and pricing structures builds a professional environment where both families and elite educators can thrive securely.

Below is the comprehensive schedule of charges, billing timelines, and payment policies governing our global services.

1. The Marketplace Service Fee

To keep our ecosystem secure, premium, and highly operational, Nuha Tutors retains a standardized marketplace service fee from all confirmed monthly tuition and service payments.

What It Covers

This fee does not include hidden costs. It directly funds continuous student-tutor matching, secure end-to-end payment processing, rigorous background and academic credential verification, and dedicated 24/7 administrative platform support.

2. Tailored Tuition & Service Rates

We do not believe in a one-size-fits-all pricing model. Tuition, daycare frameworks, and speech therapy fees are dynamically structured before official enrollment.

Variable Factors

Final monthly or hourly rates are mutually agreed upon based on the specific subject complexity, academic level (e.g., O/A Levels, IB MYP), total session duration, weekly frequency, and the specific qualifications or certifications of the assigned specialist.

3. Risk-Free Demo Classes

We ensure you find the perfect match before any financial commitment is made.

Free for Students

Trial/demo sessions arranged during the initial tutor selection process are completely free of cost for families for up to two consecutive days.

Evaluation Phase

Because these initial demo sessions function as mutual evaluation interviews, independent tutors are not financially compensated for unconfirmed demos. Compensation cycles trigger immediately upon formal client enrollment.

4. Mandatory Advance Payments & Tutor Release Timelines

To protect the operational flow and respect our educators' schedules, we follow a strict payment window:

Advance Monthly Billing

As explicitly outlined in our platform terms, full monthly fees must be deposited in advance to Nuha Tutors immediately following a successful 2-day demo class before regular sessions can resume.

Secure Tutor Payouts

To ensure absolute payment clearance and lesson verification, funds are released to independent tutors after a standard verification and processing window (typically 7 to 10 working days) following the successful completion of the scheduled monthly cycle or sessions.

5. Policy on Missed Sessions & Rescheduling

Our billing model aligns directly with scheduled and delivered educational or therapy sessions.

Timely Notice Required

If a student misses a session without providing a 12-to-24-hour prior notice, the session will be counted as consumed.

Fair Adjustments

If sessions are missed due to a tutor's unavailability or are canceled well in advance, makeup sessions will be structured. If rescheduling is completely impossible, fair financial adjustments or session roll-overs will be applied at the sole discretion of the management to ensure fairness to both parties.

6. Professional Writing & Consultation Services

Pricing for specialized writing tracks operates on a customized, project-by-project framework rather than a standard hourly tuition model.

Custom Quotes

Costs for academic writing support, proofreading, and consultations vary dynamically depending on the subject complexity, technical depth, strict deadlines, and total word count.

How to Apply

Clients looking for professional consultation can easily request a tailored, binding quote by submitting their project parameters through our official Contact Us page.
