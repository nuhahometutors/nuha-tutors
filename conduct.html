Conduct Policy

Expected behavior for all Nuha Tutors platform members.

Professional Standards

All tutors and students must maintain professional, respectful communication at all times. Harassment, discrimination, or abusive language is strictly prohibited.

Punctuality

Tutors and students are expected to attend scheduled sessions on time. Repeated lateness or no-shows may result in payment adjustments or account review.

Academic Integrity

Tutors must not complete assignments on behalf of students in ways that violate academic integrity policies. Writing services must produce original work with proper attribution.

Confidentiality

Personal information shared during tutoring must be kept confidential. Tutors must not share student data with third parties.

Reporting

Report conduct violations to our support team. All reports are investigated and appropriate action is taken.
