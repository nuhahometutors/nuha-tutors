Shortlisting Policy

How tutor shortlisting and demo selection works.

Student Selection

Shortlisting for demo classes is entirely the student's decision. Nuha Tutors presents qualified tutor profiles but cannot force students to conduct demos with every applicant.

Fair Presentation

All tutor applicants matching student requirements are presented fairly. Selection is based on student preference, subject match, availability, and profile alignment — not nationality, race, or personal factors.

Demo Classes

Demo classes are part of the evaluation process and confirmation is not guaranteed. Payment applies only once regular classes are formally confirmed.

Non-Selection

Not being shortlisted does not indicate a tutor is ineligible. It reflects the student's preference and requirement matching, similar to job application processes.
