Nuha Tutors Definitions: Understanding Our Key Terms

To ensure absolute clarity and a seamless experience for families, educators, and partners, we believe in defining the foundational terms used across all Nuha Tutors policies. These definitions form the basis of our user agreement, operational structure, and support services.

1. The Platform

Definition: "Platform" refers explicitly to Nuha Tutors International, including its official website, matching portals, automated communication tracks, social media avenues, and all associated marketplace facilitation services.

Scope: Any interaction, form submission, or data exchange occurring within these digital channels is governed directly by our platform's protocols.

2. The Tutor / Educator / Specialist

Definition: An independent, verified professional registered on Nuha Tutors who provides specialized academic tutoring, comprehensive homeschooling guidance, day-to-day childcare, speech therapy, or professional writing consultation services.

Legal Status: Tutors operate as independent service providers and contractors, utilizing the platform to discover and manage engagements with clients.

3. The Student / Client

Definition: Any individual learner, parent, or legal guardian who utilizes Nuha Tutors to seek out, communicate with, and officially hire an independent professional for educational or developmental support services.

Accountability: The client represents the primary entity responsible for coordinating goals and finalizing payment cycles for the learner.

4. The Demo Class

Definition: A complimentary, short-term trial tutoring or introductory session arranged by the platform before any long-term service commitment is made.

Duration: This evaluation phase is typically offered completely risk-free for a period of up to two consecutive days to ensure a perfect match between the student and the instructor.

5. Monthly Tuition / Confirmed Engagement

Definition: A formalized, ongoing instructional or care arrangement established after a successful demo period, featuring a mutually agreed-upon schedule, locked-in monthly fees, and specific hour durations.

Activation: This engagement is officially activated only when the required advance payment is successfully processed through our authorized channels.

6. The Marketplace Fee

Definition: The standardized administrative and operational service fee retained by Nuha Tutors from the overall structural transaction.

Purpose: This fee is utilized directly to fund rigorous background checks, continuous tutor profile vetting, communication infrastructure maintenance, payment protection systems, and ongoing client support services.
