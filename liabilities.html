Nuha Tutors Limitation of Liability: Understanding Our Legal Boundaries

At Nuha Tutors, we are dedicated to maintaining a transparent, reliable, and high-quality marketplace for education, child development, and specialized care. While we implement strict vetting and safety standards, it is essential for all users—including parents, guardians, students, and independent professionals—to understand the precise legal boundaries and limits of our liability.

By accessing our website and engaging our services, you formally agree to the liabilities and legal protections outlined below.

1. The Scope of Platform Liability

Nuha Tutors functions strictly as a premium administrative facilitator that safely matches students and families with independent educators, therapists, and caregivers.

Facilitation Boundary

Our legal responsibility is strictly confined to verifying professional credentials, running background checks, facilitating initial matches, and securing advance payment handling.

Instructional Quality & Outcomes

Nuha Tutors is not legally liable for the subjective quality of lessons delivered, specific academic outcomes, or developmental speeds.

Private Arrangements

We explicitly disclaim all liability for any disputes, financial losses, or operational disagreements that arise if a client and an educator enter into direct, private agreements that bypass our stated facilitation framework.

2. Operational Autonomy of Independent Professionals

Every tutor, homeschooling guide, speech therapist, and childcare provider registered under our network operates as an autonomous, independent contractor.

No Direct Employment

Professionals listed on this platform are not internal employees, legal agents, or direct representatives of Nuha Tutors.

Individual Accountability

Nuha Tutors cannot be held responsible or legally liable for the personal actions, behavioral conduct, or choices made by a tutor outside our agreed platform policies.

Enforcement Protocols

We take our community's safety seriously. While we are not liable for individual actions, we reserve the right to rigorously investigate any reported violations and permanently suspend offending profiles from our ecosystem.

3. Absolute Limitation of Damages

To the maximum extent permitted under applicable law, Nuha Tutors, its founders, directors, and administrative teams shall not be held liable for any:

  • Indirect, incidental, or special damages.
  • Consequential or punitive damages.
  • Loss of profits, loss of data, property damage, or personal inconveniences arising out of, or in connection with, the use of our online platform or physical in-person services.

4. Structured Dispute Resolution Process

We believe that most operational misunderstandings can be resolved through clear communication and mutual respect. In the event of a disagreement:

Report to Support First

Any dispute regarding lesson scheduling, tutor attendance, billing discrepancies, or behavioral concerns must first be formally reported through our official support channels.

Our Facilitation Role

Our administration will actively mediate and facilitate constructive communication between both parties to find an amicable solution.

Mutual Consent

Decisions involving purely academic matters, student grades, or clinical methodology require mutual consent between the professional and the student/parent, as Nuha Tutors does not dictate individual educational outcomes.
