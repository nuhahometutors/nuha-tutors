Nuha Tutors Privacy Policy: How We Protect Your Trust and Data

At Nuha Tutors, we believe that education and care thrive in an environment built on absolute trust. As a premium global platform offering personalized online and home tutoring, comprehensive home schooling support, daycare services, and specialized speech therapy, we interact with information that matters deeply to your family.

Your privacy is our utmost priority. This article outlines exactly how we collect, utilize, and safeguard your personal data, ensuring complete transparency every step of the way.

1. The Information We Collect (And Why It Matters)

To deliver a tailored experience that perfectly matches students with elite educators, we gather specific, relevant information:

For Families & Students

We collect fundamental details such as names, contact information (WhatsApp, phone numbers, and emails), the student's grade, and their specific academic goals.

For Specialized Services

When families enroll in our Speech Therapy, Home Schooling, or Special Needs Support tracks, parents may voluntarily share behavioral or developmental insights. This sensitive data is treated with the highest level of confidentiality and is used purely to customize the learning structure.

For Educators & Caregivers

To maintain our rigorous standards, we collect comprehensive profiles of our tutors, including academic credentials, proven teaching history, verified background checks, and official identification.

2. Crafting the Perfect Match: How Your Data is Used

We do not believe in automated, bulk processing. Your data is used intelligently and purposefully by our dedicated teams to:

  • Carefully screen and bridge the gap between families and the most qualified global educators.
  • Facilitate seamless communication through your preferred channels, whether you prefer coordinating via WhatsApp, a direct phone call, or email.
  • Securely manage enrollment, process subscription fees, and coordinate tailored lesson schedules.

3. Fort-Knox Security: Keeping Your Data Safe

We implement industry-standard administrative and technical barriers to ensure your data is never compromised.

Our Golden Rule: Nuha Tutors will never sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third-party marketing agencies. Period.

All background checks, tutor credentials, and client consultation notes are stored within a restricted-access, encrypted database. Furthermore, all financial transactions are processed through highly secure, globally trusted payment gateways to guarantee your financial peace of mind.

4. Transparent Sharing: What Goes Public?

We respect the boundary between professional representation and personal privacy:

Tutor Profiles

Only verified qualifications, teaching experience, general location, and parent reviews are shared with families looking for a match. Direct contact details remain hidden until a formal connection is made.

Student Privacy

Student details are never publicly listed. Tutors only receive the necessary academic context required to prepare for a successful trial session.

5. You Are in Control: Your Privacy Rights

We believe that you should always own your data. At any point during your journey with Nuha Tutors, you have the right to:

  • Access, review, or update your personal or professional profile details.
  • Opt-out of updates, newsletters, or platform announcements.
  • Request the permanent deletion of your account and all associated data from our secure systems.

Looking for More Information?

We are fully committed to keeping this policy aligned with global data protection standards. If you have any questions about how your information is handled, or if you want to request a data update, please connect with our support team through your preferred communication channel.

You may also contact us directly at NuhaTutors@gmail.com.
