Circumvention Policy

Rules regarding direct dealings outside the platform.

No Direct Dealings

Tutors and students introduced through Nuha Tutors must not circumvent the platform by arranging payments or ongoing tuition directly outside the marketplace for connections made through our services.

Why This Matters

Platform fees support student acquisition, payment security, verification, dispute resolution, and ongoing support. Circumvention undermines fairness for all platform participants.

Consequences

Violation of circumvention policy may result in immediate account termination, forfeiture of pending payments, and legal action where applicable.

Renewals

All tuition renewals for students matched through Nuha Tutors must be processed through the platform unless formally released in writing by Nuha Tutors administration.
